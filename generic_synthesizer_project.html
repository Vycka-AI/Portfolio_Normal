Noisemaker with step sequencer

This project started as a simple APC synth, which uses two NE 555 timers. You could vary pitch and tone with two potentiometers in the circuit. This was a cool project, but I wanted to upgrade it and make it sound more fun. That's why step sequencer CD4017 was added to the mix. It's rate of stepping could be controlled with another NE 555 timer. 8 out of 10 step sequencer outputs were used so it can make a beat that's more comfortable for humans to listen. Each output is fed into it's designated potentiometer and an LED for visualizing. Signal from potentiometer is fed into the APC synth, which's output is fed into a speaker and sound is heard. Buttons for each step were added so, if wanted, a capacitor could be connected in parallel for fade in effect.
